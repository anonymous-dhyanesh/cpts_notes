- `vim file` – Open file  
- `Esc + i` – Insert mode  
- `Esc` – Normal mode  
- `x` – Cut character  
- `dw` – Cut word  
- `dd` – Cut full line  
- `yw` – Copy word  
- `yy` – Copy full line  
- `p` – Paste  
- `:1` – Go to line 1  
- `:w` – Save  
- `:q` – Quit  
- `:q!` – Quit without saving  
- `:wq` – Save and quit
